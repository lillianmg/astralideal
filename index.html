About Me

About Me

Mar has always known there was more beneath the surface of things. Rooted in indigenous ancestry, she grew up with an innate understanding that the world speaks in symbols, cycles, and signs. Long before she had language for it, she was already listening.

Astrology and tarot were not discoveries for Mar. They were recognitions. A lifelong spiritual calling that had been walking alongside her, waiting for the moment she was ready to answer fully. That moment came through loss. Through a crisis so heavy she wasn't sure she would find her footing again. What she found instead was her foundation.

It was in that darkness that Mar went all in. She immersed herself in the study of astrology and tarot not as an escape, but as a lantern. The planets, the cards, the ancient symbolic language her ancestors understood long before it had Western names: all of it became the map that helped her find her way back to herself. And then forward.

She is not afraid to sit with pain, and she will not ask you to look away from yours. Mar is a living example that pain is not the end of the story. It is some of the most potent material we carry. When we stop running from it and begin to read it, it becomes fuel. It becomes direction. It alchemizes into purpose and propels you forward on your path.

Mar reads with directness, depth, and a grounded practicality that cuts through noise. She is not here to tell you what you want to hear. She is here to help you remember what you already know.
