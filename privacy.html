Privacy Policy

Effective: · AstralIdeal is committed to protecting your personal information and handling it with care, transparency, and respect for your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard information shared with us.

Information We Collect

When you contact us or book a session, we collect your name, email address, birth date and location (for natal chart work), and any information you voluntarily share in your message or during a session.

How We Use It

Your information is used solely to provide your requested reading, communicate about scheduling and session details, and send any agreed follow-up materials such as session recordings or written summaries. We do not use your data for marketing without your explicit consent.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes. Trusted service providers such as Zoom and email platforms may process certain data under their own privacy policies and confidentiality obligations.

Your Rights & Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with applicable legal obligations. You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time through our contact form. We will respond within 30 days.

Cookies & Tracking

This website uses no tracking cookies, third-party analytics scripts, or advertising pixels. We do not build behavioral profiles, retarget you, or track your activity across other websites in any way.

Pennsylvania Residents

Pennsylvania residents may have additional rights under applicable state privacy law. If you have questions about your specific rights or wish to exercise them, please reach out through our contact form.
